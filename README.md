# MaxMind Fraud Prevention - Shopware 6 Plugin
## ✨ Shopware 6 Plugin

Protect your Shopware store with **MaxMind Fraud Prevention**! Detect high-risk orders, prevent chargebacks, and analyze transactions in real-time with AI-driven fraud scoring.  

## 🚀 Why Use MaxMind?  
- **Prevent Fraud Before It Happens** – Identify fraudulent transactions before processing.  
- **Reduce Chargebacks & Losses** – Protect your revenue from fraud-related disputes.  
- **Advanced Risk Scoring** – MaxMind evaluates IP addresses, geolocation, and order behavior.  
- **Automate Fraud Prevention** – Set custom risk thresholds and take immediate action.  

### 📌 Features  
✔ **Real-Time Risk Analysis** – Instantly assess every order for fraud risk.  
✔ **Fraud Risk Scoring** – Orders receive a fraud score based on multiple risk factors.  
✔ **Automatic Order Flagging** – High-risk orders can be put on hold or canceled.  
✔ **IP & Geolocation Tracking** – Detect fraudulent locations and suspicious activity.  
✔ **Device Fingerprinting** – Identify repeat fraud attempts.  
✔ **Customizable Risk Thresholds** – Set fraud levels and automate actions.  
✔ **Chargeback Prevention** – Reduce chargebacks by blocking fraudulent transactions.  
✔ **Custom Rules & Alerts** – Get email notifications for flagged transactions.  
✔ **Chargeback Reporting** – Send fraud data to MaxMind to improve future risk analysis.  
✔ **Shopware Admin Dashboard Integration** – View fraud scores and risk insights within Shopware.
